Lucky me, my wife is British-Canadian born in England and raised in Montreal. Unless you’ve spent time in Quebec, you wouldn’t know how many great beers are brewed within that province (and not exported.) But the British connection is even better. I fell in love with English pubs when I first visited in 1992. I love the beer, the taps, the woodwork, the perfectly poured pints, and the hand painted signs with quirky names. The traditional English pub is sadly in decline, but there are still thousands of them. I’m also drawn to the incredible landscape of the British Isles, with all their stone circles, dolmans, and earthworks. Every two years, my wife and I visit England and take side trips that allow me to take pictures all day and relax in pubs at night. I have that blood in me, and I just feel at home with the accents, the pubs, the stones, and the lovely litter-free country lanes congested only by meandering sheep. I post my travel pictures on Smug Mug and Instagram.
