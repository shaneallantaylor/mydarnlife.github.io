My final years in high school were defined by cigarettes, pot, and acoustic music jamming with friends in jean jackets. We played Dylan, Dead, and New Riders of the Purple Sage. I dabbled in fingerpicking Delta Blues. We smoked pot when we could get it and I could never roll a decent joint. I went to college at Indiana University of Pennsylvania, but transferred to the University of Virginia in year two. I lived on on my own ever since the summer I graduated high school. I self-rated my intellectual abilities very high because I read all of Gravity’s Rainbow (no skimming). I studied Psychology. And most importantly - I had exquisite taste in music. Of this I am certain.
